Do you want to promote your club or organization beyond social media, but don't know how to set up a website? If so, post your information on the Redlands Orange Press website!

The Orange Press will post your information, meeting times, contact emails, and anything else you want to share, to the web for a one-time cost of $50. Regular modifications can be made at an additional cost of $10 per modification.

The website will look exactly like this webpage, but with your information instead of this promo.

Benefits:

  • No need to learn HTML, or figure out Wix
  • You will show up in Google searches
  • People won't need to log in to Facebook or Instagram to learn about you

Facebook and Instagram provide an easy platform for you to publish information, but both come with difficulties. Facebook often requires log-in information, and only allows a short section on information about you. Instagram has even less space: you can provide a short bio, and can post photo-centered updates, but the opportunity to truly tell your story is limited.

Expand your offerings! Post on social media, and on the web. Contact me at aidan@redlandsorangepress.com if interested.
