Redlands offers a lot to its residents! But it can be hard to find the group for your passions and interests. I made this site to help you find the people to bike with, read with, or do whatever else you like to do—or want to like to do!—in town. Currently, I'm focusing on adding in those events that happen on a set interval: daily, weekly or monthly. If you want to add additional events or places, you can provide the details here. You can also email me at aidan@redlandsorangepress.com.

—Aidan

Athletics

  • Redlands Ruunrs
    • This group runs Thursdays at 6:30 p.m., starting at the Downtown Train Station and ending at Escape Brewery.
  • Flowstone Climbing
    • Rock climbing gym at 1411 W State Street. Open 8am-10pm weekdays, 10am-9m weekends. Private climbing instruction slots Mondays, Wednesdays at 5pm and 6pm. Multiple yoga sessions. Full schedule online. $85 monthly. $22 day pass.
  • Sierra Club outings
    • The Sierra Club of San Gorgonio hosts intermittent outings and monthly meetings for people who want to experience the outdoors.
  • The Wolf's Den
    • A ninja warrior/obstacle course racing gym with yoga, HITT, a weight center, a chase tag center, knife throwing and pre-planned exercise routines designed by active coaches. Has activities for children. Membership rates are dependent on frequency of attendance and program. Has different programs at different times of the day. Located at 1930 S E St, San Bernardino, CA 92408.
  • YMCA of the East Valley
    • Redlands' YMCA is located at 500 E. Citrus Avenue, downtown. They offer group aquatics and exercise classes for all ages. Adult memberships are $45.
  • One Jiu Jitsu Redlands
    • Brazilian Jiu Jitsu Academy at 310 Alabama St. Evening classes. Monthly membership.

Games

  • Bowling
    • Empire Bowl has $50 lanes/shoe rentals Thursday nights for parties of four, $25 lanes at 11 a.m. Monday through Thursday and 9 p.m. Sunday through Thursday, $55 lanes on the weekend and $80 cosmic bowling lanes Friday and Saturday night.
  • Trivia night
    • Hangar 24 Brewing hosts trivia games Thursdays at 7:00 p.m.
  • Board Game Nights—Board Game Paradise
    • Board Game Paradise hosts game nights every Tuesday and Thursday at their downtown location at 109 E State St.
  • Chess in the Park
    • Board Game Paradise hosts chess matches every Sunday at noon at Ed Hales Park, downtown next to their shop. They provide boards, but you can bring your own.

Culture

  • SoCal in Film series
    • The AK Smiley Public Library hosts a movie showing at 2 p.m. every third Saturday. The showings are held in the Contempory Club, which sits behind the Lincoln Shrine.

  • Cars and Coffee

    • Car enthusiasts meet at 300 Oriental Avenue at 9 a.m. every third Saturday.
  • Tuesday night film classics
    • Harkins Theaters hosts a viewing of a classic movie 7 p.m. every Tuesday.
  • Memoir Book Club
    • The Frugal Frigate hosts an memoir book club for adults at 6:30 p.m. every second Thursday.
  • Fiction Book Club
    • The Frugal Frigate hosts an fiction book club for adults at 6:30 p.m. every third Thursday.
  • Nonfiction Book Club
    • The Frugal Frigate hosts an nonfiction book club for adults at 6:30 p.m. every fourth Thursday.
  • Friends Book Clubs
    • The Friends of the A.K. Smiley Public Library host three book clubs. The Eclectic Readers meet every 2nd Tuesday at 7 p.m. The Third Wednesday Book Club meets at 2:30 p.m. The Fourth Monday Book Club meets at 11 a.m. Contact information for the leaders of the clubs and their current book is available online.
  • Tour Kimberly Crest
    • Docents give $15 tours of the Kimberly Crest interior Thursday, Friday and Sunday afternoons, and free tours of the gardens 10 a.m. to 4 p.m. Friday and Sunday.

Arts

  • Artlands' Monthly Art Fair
    • The Artlands hosts an art fair every 1st Saturday from 6-9 p.m.
  • Art Association Classes
    • The Redlands Art Association hosts clay, pastels, oils, watercolors and drawing classes and one-day workshops for adults. The classes, which include four sessions, range from $80 to $100. The one-day workshops range from $55 to $75. Check their website for the current schedule.
  • Redlands Pottery Project
    • The Redlands Pottery Project is open 11 a.m. to 8 p.m. Wednesday through Sunday. They also provide 90-minute intro classes at 6:00 p.m. Fridays and 4:00 p.m. Saturdays. Monthly memberships are $175, and the intro class is $80.

Music

  • The Vault's Open Mic Night

    • The Vault on Vine Street hosts open mic every Monday.

  • Live music

    • The Three Stags on Orange St. hosts live music on weekends. Check their website for the schedule.

  • Karoake

    • The Royal Falconer hosts karaoke every Monday and Thursday night.

Volunteer

  • Helping Hands Pantry
    • The Helping Hands Pantry needs volunteers to manage their warehouse Sunday-Friday 7:00 a.m. to noon, at 2205 S. Artesia St., San Bernardino. They also need delivery drivers, office volunteers and volunteer gardeners.
  • Family Service Association of Redlands
    • The Family Service Association needs volunteers to help with the breakfast program, the clothing room, and the food pantry. Their volunteer application lists shift times.
  • San Bernardino County Museum
    • The museum has flexible hours for volunteers to manage their collections. All people over 15 years old can volunteer, as long as they can meet six hours a month.
  • Habitat for Humanity
    • Habitat for Humanity needs construction volunteers, hospitality volunteers, and stocking volunteers.
  • Boys and Girls Club of Greater Redlands-Riverside
    • This nonprofit takes volunteers to mentor children.
  • Assistance League of Redlands
    • The Assistance League, along with its teenage division, Assisteens, regularly does community service events.

Civics

  • City Council meetings
    • The City Council meets on the first and third Tuesday of the month.
  • School board meetings
    • Redlands' school board meets on the second and fourth Tuesday of each month, in the Board Room, 25 West Lugonia, Redlands, at 6:00 p.m.
  • Planning commission
    • Redlands' Planning Commission meets on the 2nd and 4th Tuesday of each month, at the City Hall, at 4:00 p.m.

Youth events

  • Preschool reading time
    • 30-minute long readings at the AK Smiley Library. 10:15 a.m every Tuesday.
  • Pajama story time
    • Pajama-friendly children reading time at AK Smiley Library. 7:00-7:30 p.m every Wednesday.
  • Frugal Frigate children's storytime
    • The Frugal Frigate hosts story time for children 10:30 a.m.-11:00 a.m. every Saturday.
  • Barnes and Noble storytime
    • The Citrus Plaza Barnes & Noble hosts storytime for elementary school children at 11:00 a.m. every Saturday.
